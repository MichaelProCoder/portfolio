About Me: MICHAEL HANSON

BIOGRAPHY

EARLY LIFE

Born in Las Vegas, Nevada 2006, Michael has always been an avid creator. From NFT's to suits of armor to truck camper shells he can make it all. His creativity is second to none. From a young age he play outdoors and explored nature. He moved to Oregon at age 5 in 2011. He excelled in his classes and was always the top of his class. By highschool, Michael was his school chapter's NHS president and #1 Varsity Pole Vaulter on his Highschool team. He graduated top 5 of his class out of 70 in June of 2024. He went on to travel abroad for a month in Italy to study Medieval History during the summer.

CURRENT

Now Michael is enrolled at the University of Oregon and set to graduate in June 2028. He is determined to possibly graduate in three years, we shall see. He is currenty majoring in Business Administration but wants to double major in Art as well. Currently holding a GPA of 4.15, Michael is an academic weapon in school. After only 2 terms in college Michael has over 58 college credits. Bro is cooking...

PLANS FOR FUTURE

The future is looking bright for bro and Michael says he wants to create a business in armor. Using his BFA in Business Admin, Michael will run a business and create works of art to sell using his BFA in ART. Michael hopes to buy rural land and move far away from the city. Michael will create works of art by a lake and become filthy rich to be able to make art and give back to the people who helped him along the way.
